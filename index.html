The Institution

University College of Engineering Nagercoil was established in the year 2009 and it is a constituent college of Anna University, Chennai. Presently, the college offers six undergraduate programmes in the departments of Civil, CSE, ECE, EEE, IT and Mechanical Engineering.

know More

The Department

The Department of Mechanical Engineering was established in 2009. The department offers B.E programmes in Mechanical Engineering (Tamil and English Medium). The department has well experienced and qualified faculties with various specializations such as Energy Engineering, Thermal Engineering, Aeronautical Engineering, Production Engineering and Design Engineering.
